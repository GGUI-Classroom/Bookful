How to Use Bookful

Create checkouts first, then add students and books as needed

This page walks you through the fastest workflow: start a checkout, add missing students or books as you go, and stay on top of overdue books.

Step 1

Start with a checkout

Click New Checkout first. Pick the student and book if they already exist, then confirm.

Step 2

Add students and books as you go

If something is missing during checkout, add the student or book and continue without changing your flow.

Step 3

Review dashboard alerts

Use the dashboard to spot overdue items and quickly see current classroom lending activity.

Step 4

Mark returns immediately

When books come back, use Mark as Returned so records stay accurate and current.

Step 5

Use books and records for accountability

Use the Books tab to add or manage titles anytime, then open Records to track active and previous checkouts.

Teacher Tip

If the checkout date does not match your local date, update it before confirming. Bookful asks for confirmation so you can catch timezone mismatches before saving.
